# Plumbline — Experimentation Logbook

Two lines per cycle. External memory for the methodology itself.

Format:

## YYYY-MM-DD — Feature name

- **Status:** in-progress | complete | abandoned
- **Applied:** which KPIs did you actually use? (linting / surfacing / catalog / parking-lot)
- **Result:** what did you observe? overhead, surprise, friction, value.

Methodology gaps surfaced during cycles are recorded in `parking-lot.md`, not here. The
logbook tracks *use* of Plumbline; the parking lot tracks *open questions about*
Plumbline.
