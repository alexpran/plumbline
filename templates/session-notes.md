# Plumbline — Session Notes

> **Scratchpad of methodological reasoning.**
> Implicit insights, partial patterns, and intermediate decisions that emerged during use
> but have not yet been promoted to formal artifacts (DDs, KPI rules, catalog categories).

## Purpose

Catalog, logbook, and parking-lot capture observable events (drifts, cycle uses, open
questions). Session notes capture the **reasoning around** those events — the
micro-patterns, the half-formed intuitions, the connections made in real time that
informed decisions but never reached an artifact of their own.

Without this file, this material lives only in the session memory of the AI and is lost
when the session ends. Session notes preserve it across sessions, so future iterations of
Plumbline have access to the accumulated reasoning, not just the accumulated decisions.

## Format

One section per session, dated. Within each session, free-form notes organized by topic.
No required structure beyond dating and topic grouping. The point is to fix material on
disk, not to format it perfectly.

Notes are not promoted, abandoned, or evaluated within this file. They simply exist until
a future consolidation session decides what becomes a DD, what enters a KPI spec, what
goes to parking-lot, what fades.
